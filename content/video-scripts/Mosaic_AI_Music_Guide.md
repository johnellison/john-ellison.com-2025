# Mosaic AI Music Guide: AI Transformation

This guide defines the standard inputs for the **Mosaic AI Music Tile**. Music is the emotional "glue" of the video; it must feel expensive, cinematic, and intentional‚Äînever generic "background music."

Use these settings to generate a custom score that aligns with the "Moody Premium" and "Interstellar" brand aesthetic.

*   **Intelligent Video Analysis**: `ON` (Allowed, but **Override** the text inputs below).

## üéº Master Prompts by Section

Choose the "Vibe" that aligns with the specific video segment you are processing.

### 1. The "Vision" (Intro / Philosophy / The 8-Month Window)
*   **The Vibe**: "Interstellar", Awe, Vastness, Deep Time.
*   **Prompt**:
    > "Cinematic sci-fi score, Hans Zimmer style, Interstellar vibes. Ethereal wide synth pads, magnificent deep cello swells, slow building tension. Sense of awe, discovery, and vast scale. No percussion."
*   **Genre**: `Cinematic` or `Ambient`
*   **Mood**: `Inspirational` or `Mysterious`
*   **Intensity**: `Medium` (Authorized weight)
*   **BPM**: `60` (Slow and heavy)

### 2. The "Problem" (Market Friction / Stuck / Chaos)
*   **The Vibe**: "Social Network", Tension, Ticking Clock, Anxiety.
*   **Prompt**:
    > "Tense modern investigative score. Minimalist pizzicato strings, low ticking clock rhythm, subtle dissonance. Uneasy, serious, analytical. Digital glitch textures in the background."
*   **Genre**: `Modern Classical` or `Electronic`
*   **Mood**: `Suspenseful` or `Serious`
*   **Intensity**: `Low` (Simmering tension, leaves room for voiceover)
*   **BPM**: `120` (Fast pulse, but quiet mix)

### 3. The "Solution" (Flow / Adoption / Momentum)
*   **The Vibe**: "Organized", Flow State, Warm, Propulsive.
*   **Prompt**:
    > "Propulsive, optimistic organic electronica. Warm analog synths, rhythmic marimba patterns, driving but subtle beat. Flow state, clarity, productivity, solving the puzzle. Tycho or Bonobo style."
*   **Genre**: `Electronica` or `Downtempo`
*   **Mood**: `Focus` or `Optimistic`
*   **Intensity**: `High` (Driving energy)
*   **BPM**: `110` (Active walking pace)
