## ðŸŽ¬ Universal Prompt Framework

To "break" the generic AI video look, use this structured prompting method for every generation:

1.  **SCENE**: One sentence summary of action.
2.  **VISUALS**: Lighting, texture, color palette (The "Look").
3.  **CAMERA**: Movement, lens choice, angle (The "Feel").
4.  **ATMOSPHERE**: Mood, haze, particle density.

## ðŸŽ¨ Style 1: Abstract Transformation (Primary for Intros/Concepts)

*Adapts "Abstract High-Concept" for motion. Use for "The 8-Month Window", "Regenerative Wisdom", and high-level strategy.*

*   **Motion Theory**: Fluid, evolving, merging. Things should *grow* or *flow*, not just sit there.
*   **Color Palette**: Deep charcoal background, bioluminescent **Electric Blue**, **Deep Purple**, and **Regenerative Green**.

### Master Prompt Template
> **SCENE**: Abstract visualization of [CONCEPT, e.g., data streams merging into a tree].
> **VISUALS**: High-fidelity digital art, organic patterns made of glowing light fibers, deep charcoal background with bioluminescent [COLOR] accents.
> **CAMERA**: Slow, majestic fly-through or push-in. Smooth, floating camera movement. Macro details evolving into macro structures.
> **ATMOSPHERE**: Ethereal, mystical but technological, shallow depth of field, tiny floating light particles.

### Example: "The Unified System"
> **SCENE**: A fractured mechanical system seamlessly reassembling itself into a glowing perfection.
> **VISUALS**: Metallic shards floating in a void, glowing with electric blue edges, slowly magnetizing toward a center core to form a perfect, pulsing sphere of light. Deep charcoal void.
> **CAMERA**: Slow rotational orbit (dolly arc), focusing on the moment of connection.
> **ATMOSPHERE**: Cinematic, precision, high-tech, 24fps.

## ðŸ’¼ Style 2: Moody Premium (Enterprise/Governance)

*Adapts "Moody Premium" for motion. Use for "The Market", "Governance", "Security", and "Enterprise" topics.*

*   **Motion Theory**: Heavy, stable, deliberate. Slow pans, subtle rack focus (changing focus from foreground to background). **NO shaky cam.**
*   **Color Palette**: Deep charcoal, subtle **Cyan** & **Violet** rim lights. Cold but expensive.

### Master Prompt Template
> **SCENE**: Cinematic documentary shot of [SUBJECT, e.g., a modern boardroom strategy session].
> **VISUALS**: Dimly lit modern office, glass walls, reflections. Deep charcoal shadows. Subjects are serious, focused, wearing smart-casual/premium attire.
> **CAMERA**: Static tripod shot with shallow depth of field. Subtle rack focus from a foreground object (e.g., a pen, a blurred screen) to the person speaking.
> **ATMOSPHERE**: Serious, heavy, "Succession" style, atmospheric haze, premium film grain.

### Example: "Governance Clarity"
> **SCENE**: A serious review of a security dashboard in a glass-walled conference room.
> **VISUALS**: Close up of a diverse professional pointing at a large screen (screen is blurred/abstract generic UI). Deep charcoal suit, serious expression. Reflection of the data streams in the glass table.
> **CAMERA**: Slow, confident dolly in. Very subtle.
> **ATMOSPHERE**: High-stakes business, quiet authority, cinematic lighting with cyan rim light.

## â˜• Style 3: Hipster Startup (Culture/Friction)

*Adapts "Hipster Startup SF Vibe" for motion. Use for "The Problem", "Internal Friction", and "Human Adoption".*

*   **Motion Theory**: Handheld, documentary, "The Office" (UK) but prettier. Slight breathe/sway in the camera. Authentic human micro-movements (nervous tapping, sighing, leaning back).
*   **Color Palette**: Warm daylight, office plants, wood tones, **Teal** accents.

### Master Prompt Template
> **SCENE**: Candid documentary moment of [SUBJECT, e.g., a team struggling with a new tool].
> **VISUALS**: Bright modern startup office, natural sunlight, plants in background. Messy desks (coffee cups, sticky notes). Subjects look tired, confused, or skeptically discussing.
> **CAMERA**: Handheld aesthetic (slight jitter/sway), looking over someone's shoulder (OTS).
> **ATMOSPHERE**: Authentic, raw, "Mission District" startup vibe, relatable frustration.

### Example: "Internal Resistance"
> **SCENE**: A team member rolling their eyes and leaning back in a chair during a meeting.
> **VISUALS**: Startup office, sun flare through window. Person crosses arms, looking skeptical at a whiteboard. Colleague in background trying to explain.
> **CAMERA**: Handheld medium shot, slight drift.
> **ATMOSPHERE**: Tension, burnout, authentic human friction, warm tones but stressed mood.
