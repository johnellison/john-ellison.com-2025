# Mosaic Motion Graphics Guide: AI Transformation

This guide defines the standard inputs for the **Mosaic Motion Graphics Tile** to ensure all generated overlays and animations align with the "Moody Premium" and "Regenerative" aesthetics of the AI Transformation ecosystem.

Use these settings to prevent generic, "YouTuber-style" pop-ups and ensure an enterprise-grade, cinematic finish.

### 1. "What kind of motion graphics would you like?" (The Prompt)

Do not use simple prompts like "cool graphics." Use this **Master Prompt** structure to force the AI into our style guidelines:

> **Master Prompt:**
> "Create premium, cinematic **documentary-style** motion graphics.
> **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, high-end 'Vox-style' data visualizations.
> **Color Palette:** Strictly strictly monochrome (White/Black) with **Electric Blue (#4F46E5)** and **Teal** accents for data points.
> **Typography:** Clean Sans-Serif (Inter/Helvetica style), high contrast.
> **Graphic Types:**
> 1. **Lower Thirds:** Clean, frosted glass or deep charcoal backgrounds for speaker names (e.g., 'John Ellison - AI Transformation Expert').
> 2. **Data Overlays:** Line graphs and bar charts floating in 3D space during 'Market' sections.
> 3. **Kinetic Typography:** Highlight key phrases ('8-Month Window', 'Governance', 'ROI') in center-screen, large impactful text.
> **Negative Constraints:** NO cartoons, NO emojis, NO bouncy 'retention editing' styles, NO bright red/yellow primary colors. Keep it serious, authoritative, and elegant."

### 2. Checkbox: "Only generate full screen graphics"
*   **Default:** `Unchecked` (We want overlays on top of the VEO video).
*   **Exception:** Check this `ON` if the video is purely a voiceover with no background footage, forcing Mosaic to create a full visual story.
