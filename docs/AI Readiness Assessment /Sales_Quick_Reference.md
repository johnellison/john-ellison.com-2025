## üé¨ DISCOVERY CONVERSATION (60 Minutes)

### **Minutes 0-5: Context Setting**
*"Thanks for taking the time. We work with organizations looking to move beyond AI pilots to actual business impact. Our assessment identifies what's blocking you in 2 weeks. Today, I want to understand your situation and see if that would be valuable."*

### **Minutes 5-15: Their Situation**
- "What's your current state with AI? Pilots? In production?"
- "What's working? What's not?"
- "What are you hoping to achieve in 12-24 months?"
- "Who else is involved in AI decisions?"

### **Minutes 15-30: Understanding Their Gaps**
- "Are you more blocked by data, talent, organizational readiness, or something else?"
- "Have you had any failed initiatives? What happened?"
- "What's the biggest frustration right now?"

### **Minutes 30-40: Positioning**
*"Here's what we're seeing with organizations like yours..."*
- Share relevant data point
- "We run a 2-week assessment that identifies your specific blockers, costs to fix, and a clear 12-month roadmap."
- "Most organizations discover 2-3 blockers they weren't aware of."

### **Minutes 40-50: Building Interest**
- "If we could identify exactly what's blocking you and map a path to unblock it in 12 months, would that be useful?"
- "What would need to be true for this to be a good use of your time and budget?"

### **Minutes 50-60: Next Steps**
- **For 40-60 orgs:** "Let's run a 2-hour strategy workshop (complimentary for serious organizations). We'll assess your situation, identify blockers, and map your 12-month plan."
- **For 0-40 orgs:** "Let's start with a 2-week assessment to understand your foundation."
